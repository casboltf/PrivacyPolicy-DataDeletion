    <h1>Data Deletion Instructions</h1>

    <p>Our App does not store any user data. If you wish to disconnect your social media accounts and delete your data, please follow these steps:</p>

    <ol>

        <li>Open the App on your device.</li>

        <li>Navigate to the settings or account section within the App.</li>

        <li>Select the option to sign out of your social media accounts.</li>
